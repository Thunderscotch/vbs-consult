VBS Process Excellence Consulting, founded by Vinoth, Bala & Suresh, is a startup engineering consultancy specializing in Pressure Die Casting (PDC) Process Excellence, Automotive Quality Systems, and Manufacturing Performance Improvement.

We support automotive and engineering manufacturers in achieving stable processes, reducing defects, and aligning operations with global standards such as IATF 16949, VDA 6.3, and ISO 9001:2015.

Sheet Metal Design Support is provided as an additional engineering service, combining DFM expertise with precision fabrication feasibility for versatile manufacturing solutions.
